📱 Platform-Specific Setup

Instagram:

  • Profile pic: Circular
  • Cover photo: Use competitor comparison or feature graphic
  • Highlights: "Features," "Pricing," "Testimonials" (when you get them)
  • Post 3x week minimum

TikTok:

  • Profile pic: Circular
  • Bio: Same as Instagram
  • Content: Short videos (15-60 sec) about booking problems & solutions
  • Post 3-5x week for algorithm boost

Facebook:

  • Profile pic: Circular or square
  • Cover photo: Large hero image or feature showcase
  • Create a business page (not personal)
  • Post 3-4x week

  • Behind the Scenes: Building process, team updates
